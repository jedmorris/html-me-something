Living in Germany

My 3-Year Adventure Living in Germany

This webpage is designed to provide an overview of some of the different countries I visited while living in Germany for the past three years. I visited 17 countries in total but here are the four I spent the most time in. Enjoy!

Countries I Visited

  1. Germany
  2. France
  3. United Kingdom
  4. Italy

Germany

I lived in Stuttgart, Germany for three years with my wife Jessica and our border collie Sunshine. Suttgart served as our 'home base' as we traveled throughout Europe.

France

Jessica and I were able to visit Paris several times. The first time we visited Paris, I had a perception that it was overrated but I soon discovered that I was very wrong. Paris quickly became one of our favorite destinations!

United Kingdom

I was able to visit the U.K., and London specifically, twice. The second time was during Christmas and the decorations were incredible!
